Frequently asked questions

  • Question:
    How much a gaming computer costs?
  • Answer:
    5 000 SEK (€500) is the very minimum I would recommend. For 10 000 SEK (€1000) you can get a very decent gaming computer. 15 000 SEK (€1500) would give you a high-end gaming PC.

  • Question:
    How much memory (RAM) do I need for gaming?
  • Answer:
    For lag-free gaming, you would need 16 GB of RAM. It is recommended to step up to 32 GB of RAM if you plan to do some video editing or live streaming.

  • Question:
    Intel Xeon is a server CPU, why do you use it for gaming computers?
  • Answer:
    Intel Xeon processors are designed for expensive data centers. Many Xeon processors have identical specifications when compared to Core i7 and Core i9 variants, but available for less money.

  • Question:
    Do you think I can upgrade my computer?
  • Answer:
    Yes, most of the computers can be upgraded, which is cheaper than building a completely new one.

  • Question:
    Can you help me overclock my computer?
  • Answer:
    No. Overclocking is not a free performance. By forcing your PC out of specs you may lose the official warranty and permanently damage your computer.
